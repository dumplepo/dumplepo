# About Me

I'm an AI Engineer with around 8 years of experience developing machine learning, computer vision, and AI automation systems. My work focuses on building scalable AI solutions that can run reliably in production
environments. I combine deep learning knowledge with full-stack engineering to develop end-to-end systems, covering everything from model training to deployment and integration. My main areas of experience include
large language models (LLMs), natural language processing (NLP), and enterprise AI applications. I've also had the opportunity to collaborate with startups, helping translate advanced AI technologies into practical solutions that address real business needs.
